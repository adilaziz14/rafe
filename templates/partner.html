About Us

ABC Marriage Lawn is a premium wedding venue located in the heart of the city. Our lawn is spacious and can accommodate up to 500 guests. With our state-of-the-art lighting and sound system, decorative stage, and comfortable seating for the couple and their families, we provide the perfect setting for your special day. Our professional catering services offer a variety of menu options to suit your preferences, and our experienced event planners will assist you in planning and executing your wedding.
